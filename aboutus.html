WHY TECH MENTORS

TECH MENTORS partners with more than 200 leading universities and companies to
bring flexible, affordable, job-relevant online learning to individuals and
organizations worldwide. We offer a range of learning opportunities—from
hands-on projects and courses to job-ready certificates and degree programs.

OUR STORY

TECH MENTORS was founded by Mishal Morris in 2020 with a vision of providing life-
transforming learning experiences to learners around the world. Today, TECH MENTORS is a global
online learning platform that offers anyone, anywhere, access to online courses and degrees
from leading universities and companies. TECH MENTORS received B Corp certification in February
2021, which means that we have a legal duty not only to our shareholders, but to also make a
positive impact on society more broadly, as we continue our efforts to reduce barriers to world-
class education for all.

82 million learners, 100+ Fortune 500 companies, and more than 6,000 campuses, businesses,
 and governmentscome to TECH MENTORS to access world-class learning—anytime, anywhere.
